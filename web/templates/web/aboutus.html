About Us

Our company works with small holder Bee farmers to improve the health and safety of honey bee's is our priority. We improve the livelihoods os small Beekeepers association by creating women beekeepers.

We work with women beekeepers by providing training in morden beekeeping technologies to save the life of bees and safeguarding the habit of honey bees. Our bees are not exposed to cormecial agriculture that apply pestcides; We mantain and tracing the whole process from the comb cells up to the packages.
